Q: Where is the nearest airport?

A: Shannon is the closest international airport. The B&B is 50 miles / 80 Km from Shannon airport and about a 2-hour drive from Dublin airport.

Q: What is the best transport option to get to Galway?

A: Hire Car or Taxi are the best options. Taxi or Private car service can be arranged with notice. From Dublin, you could take the train to Galway City and take a taxi or bus to the house. There is a bus stop 60yards away on the main road.

Q: What time is check-in?

A: The regular check-in times are daily from 12pm - 6pm. Please make the B&B aware if you plan to arrive outside of these hours.

Q: What time is breakfast served?

A: Breakfast is served from 7.30am weekdays and 7am weekends. Last breakfast orders 10:30am. If you would like a later breakfast just let us know in advance.

Q: What is included in the room rate?

A: All rooms in the B&B offer the following to its guests: luxuriously furnished, non-smoking, own private bathrooms with full size baths, separate walk-in tropical rain showers, walnut wooden floors, central heating, iron and ironing board, hair dryer, all with 32" to 40" flat T.V. and tea/coffee making facilities. Full breakfast, free 24-hour wireless broadband Internet access throughout, free private off street car park and all taxes are included in rate.

Q: Do you have a swimming pool?

A: Sorry we don’t have a swimming pool but the Atlantic Ocean is only a 5min walk away.

Q: Do you arrange tours and excursions?

A: Sightseeing tours can be arranged at the B&B at a discount for: Connemara, Cliffs of Moher, The Aran Islands and a bus tour of Galway City. All tickets can be purchased on arrival.

Q: What facilities are nearby?

A: Facilities nearby to the B&B include: Eyre Square Shopping Centre, three sandy beaches, spectacular coastal walks, a variety of restaurants within walking distance, friendly traditional Irish music pubs, horse-riding, golfing, fishing, and a variety of water sports. Bikes are available for hire.

Q: Do you have a Lunch or Dinner menus/options?

A: Sorry we don’t serve lunch or dinner. However, there are several award-winning restaurants nearby. Please ask at check-in for recommendations.

Q: Can you smoke in the house?

A: Sorry the house and rooms are non-smoking. We do provide an outside sheltered area for smokers.
