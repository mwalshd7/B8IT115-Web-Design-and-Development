The House

Ocean View House is a stunning 3 story period residence with spectacular gardens and amazing views of Galway Bay and the surrounding area. The house was built in the late 1800's and was extensively refurbished 10 years ago.The gardens have direct access to a private beach for walks or maybe for a swim if your brave.


Guests have access to two drawing rooms with stunning views. There is also a library, a TV room and a games room to keep guests entertained.


Breakfast is served in the dinning room at the rear of the house. During the summer season we also serve breakfast outside (weather permitting) on the terrace looking over the bay.


The Rooms

Ocean View B&B offers 4 rooms to our guests, each one having a special character and charm. All rooms have a private bathroom. The guest rooms have been recently upgraded to high quality standards while being sensitive to the character of the house.


The Atlantic Way Suite

The Wild Atlantic Way Suite - Single Room – sleeps max of 1 person. Own private balcony


The Moher Suite

The Moher Suite - Double or Twin Room – sleeps max of 2 people.


The West Suite

The West Suite - Triple Room – sleeps max of 3 people. Three single beds or one double bed and one single bed provided.


The Connacht Suite

The Connacht Suite - Family Room – sleeps max of 2 adults and 2 children. One double and two single beds. Cots available.
